Five questions to take into any deployment

Which error type matters more in this system, and why? Anchor the answer to the specific people affected, not to general principles.

Who bears the false negative? If you cannot name them or describe their situation, you do not yet have enough to make the decision.

What recourse do they have? Honest assessment: Is it fast, accessible, and realistic for an ordinary person without legal or technical support?

Who owns the deployment decision? A name, not a team.

What would make you withdraw the system? Decide this before deployment, when the pressure to keep it running is not yet a factor.
