# Fairness, Risk, and Governance

## Universal default

Use this skill for medium/high-impact decisions or regulated environments.

## Procedure

1. Impact and harm assessment
- Identify affected users and potential harms.
- Classify risk level and escalation path.

2. Fairness and bias review
- Define protected or sensitive groups if applicable.
- Evaluate disparity metrics where policy requires.

3. Explainability and transparency
- Define explanation depth needed by audience.
- Define documentation and communication requirements.

4. Governance controls
- Approval workflow and accountability mapping.
- Audit trail requirements and retention policy.

5. Compliance alignment
- Map controls to legal or policy obligations.

## Output format

- Risk classification
- Harm and fairness assessment
- Required controls
- Governance checklist
- Ship/no-ship recommendation

## Project adaptation notes

- Remove this skill only where governance explicitly permits.
- Add domain laws and internal policy references.
- Add human-in-the-loop checkpoints for critical decisions.
